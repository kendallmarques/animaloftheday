Komodo Dragon

Indonesia

Fast Facts

Largest Lizards. Can reach up to 10 feet in length and weigh up to 200 pounds.

Carnivores who like to eat pigs, deer, snakes, fish, and water.

Takes 8 to 9 years to mature, and estimated to live up to 30 years.
